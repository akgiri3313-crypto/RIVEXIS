## DESIGN SYSTEM (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

**Color palette:**
- Background: #080808 (near black)
- Surface: #0f0f0f / #141414
- Border: rgba(255,255,255,0.06)
- Primary accent: #00FF94 (electric mint green)
- Secondary accent: #7B61FF (violet)
- Text primary: #F5F5F5
- Text muted: #6B7280

**Typography:**
- Display/headlines: "Syne" (Google Font) — bold, geometric
- Body: "DM Sans" (Google Font) — clean, readable
- Monospace accents: "JetBrains Mono" — for stats, labels, badges

**Visual details:**
- Subtle radial gradient glow behind hero (accent color, very low opacity ~0.08)
- Very faint dot-grid or noise texture on background
- Cards: glassmorphism — background rgba(255,255,255,0.03), border rgba(255,255,255,0.07), backdrop-filter blur(12px)
- Dividers: thin 1px lines at opacity 0.06
- CTA button: solid accent green, black text, pill-shaped, slight glow on hover
- NO stock photos. NO illustrations of people. Use SVG icons, geometric shapes, abstract glows only.
- Generous white space. Think Linear.app density.

**Animations:**
- On page load: staggered fade-up for hero elements (CSS keyframes, 0.1s delays)
- Scroll-triggered fade-in for each section (use IntersectionObserver)
- Hover states on cards: subtle border brightening + translateY(-2px)
- CTA button: pulsing glow animation on idle
